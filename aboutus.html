About Us

Welcome to PG computer stores, your one-stop online shop for all your computer parts needs. Our mission is to provide you with top-quality components, exceptional customer service, and competitive prices. Whether you're building a new PC, upgrading an existing one, or looking for specific parts, we've got you covered.

Who We Are

Founded in 2019, PG computer stores was born out of a passion for technology and a desire to make high-quality computer parts accessible to everyone. Our team consists of tech enthusiasts, experienced professionals, and customer service experts dedicated to helping you find the perfect components for your setup.

What We Offer

At PG computer stores, we offer a wide range of computer parts, including: - Processors: From entry-level to high-performance CPUs - Motherboards: For every form factor and chipset - Graphics Cards: For gamers, creators, and professionals - Memory: High-speed RAM for all your multitasking needs - Storage: SSDs, HDDs, and external drives - Power Supplies: Reliable units to power your build - Cooling Solutions: Air and liquid cooling options - Peripherals and Accessories: Keyboards, mice, cases, and more
